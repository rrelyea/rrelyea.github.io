VisionWalk 2020 - QTeam - Donate | Walk | Watch

Hope you are well in this difficult year! We wanted to update you about the 2020 VisionWalk. This will be our 5th year participating on Saturday, October 24th. This year, not surprisingly, it is a virtual walk and fundraiser for the Foundation Fighting Blindness (FFB).

We're raising money and asking friends, family, and colleagues to do any or all of the following:

*       Donate. Help advance research towards treatments and cures for Stargardt = consider donating for our QTeam2020 here. (Matching your gift)

*       Walk. Do a walk or other activity in honor of the VisionWalk, on the 24th or before. Send us pics if you'd like-and tell us if you want us to share them (or NOT?) with the Foundation for them to share too. (normally, all the participants do a 5K walk at Magnuson Park in Seattle)

*       Watch. Join ~20 different FFB chapters from across the nation on an online event on Oct 24th at 9am PT - VisionWalk-Foundation Fighting Blindness (fightingblindness.org/VisionWalk)

 

Hope with Research Advances

People with Stargardt disease are watching and supporting great progress on many fronts in research. No major breakthroughs yet though!!

If you are very interested in going deep into detail, I've published a list of 5 pre-clinical efforts and 36 clinical studies (some just getting started, some in progress, and some complete) on: https://rrelyea.github.io/stargardt/stargardt-2020-10.html

We are thankful for your support of us and Foundation Fighting Blindness - which continues to drive these advances forward!

Thanks to all!
