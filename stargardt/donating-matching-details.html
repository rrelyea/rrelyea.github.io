A) Donating via give.fightingblindness.org site (and QTeam page)

  1. go to QTeam2020 donation page
  2. click donate
  3. choose an amount
  4. fill out form with info
  5. click give now at bottom of page

...then Match that donation via Microsoft's employee match

This part only works if you are a Microsoft Employee! Please match with your employer.
  1. Go to: Foundation Fighting Blindness' page on Microsoft give
  2. click on "+ request a match" (white button)
  3. fill out form
  4. in “comments for charity” field, say:
    “Please route credit for funds to: VisionWalk, Washington Chapter, Team: QTeam, Captain: Rob Relyea, CC: Michele Goldfarb-Shapiro”
  5. click confirm match request…
  6. once done...please tell me you requested a match...I can keep an eye out for it.

or B) Donating and Matching via Microsoft's employee giving

This only works if you are a Microsoft Employee!
  1. Go to: Foundation Fighting Blindness' page on Microsoft give
  2. click on "+ donate now" (blue button)
  3. enter amount, and other data
  4. click "leave a comment" and enter the following in the textbox that appears:
    “Please route credit for funds to: VisionWalk, Washington Chapter, Team: QTeam, Captain: Rob Relyea, CC: Michele Goldfarb-Shapiro”
  5. click "next: confirm match request", etc...
  6. once done...please tell me you donated and requested a match through Microsoft...I can keep an eye out for it. If you tell me the amount, I can try to have the team get credit now on the QTeam page, anonymously or with your name.
